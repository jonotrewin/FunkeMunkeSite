About Us

Just Two Devs
Making Chaos

Funke Munke is an indie game studio built by two people who refused to make normal games.

We craft weird stories and fun experiences — games that make you laugh, think, and occasionally question reality. From time-loop party puzzles to surreal narrative RPGs, if it's unexpected, it's probably ours.

We're small, scrappy, and we ship games that feel like nothing else out there.
